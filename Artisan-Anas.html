Senaat Beladi's Got Talent

Said Aabilla

Manufacture: Moderne

Né: December 8, 1997; Marrakech

Categorie: Cuir & Sculpture de bois

Influence par: Davinci, lmaalem Hassan

Institition: Travaux pratique, FEZ

Diego Rivera was an outspoken member of the Mexican communist party and husband to painter Frida Kahlo. Rivera began drawing at the age of three, one year after the death of his twin brother Carlos. His father built him a studio with chalkboard and canvas on the walls to keep him from drawing on the walls and furniture. At the age of ten, he studied at the Academy of San Carlos, and at the age of 21 was sponsored by the governor of Veracruz to study art in Europe. While in Europe, he traveled and studied in Madrid, Paris, and Italy, and befriended many of the famous Paris artists of the time
